Reasons I want to Code

After Covid, inflation being on the rise and an unstable economy, finding security in knowing that I could provide for myself and future family if need be during another crisis is essential. Thats why my main reasons for wanting to code as a living are the ability to work from home, and good pay.

Work from Home

Being able to work from home is very important to me. I deal with plenty of social anxiety and other personal problems, that if given the privacy of being able to be at home during work, makes dealing wit those problems a lot more managable. I also deal with a slipping vertebrae, so the ablity to not have to stand during work is also a plus. Commutes and gas prices add to this as well.
